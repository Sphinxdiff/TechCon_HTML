History

TechCon started in 2008 as a local meetup in Silicon Valley, driven by a group of tech pioneers and innovators looking for a platform to share ideas. The founders—software developers, hardware engineers, and tech entrepreneurs—envisioned an event that would foster collaboration across different tech sectors.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and growth in the global technology community. We are dedicated to providing a platform where industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts can come together to share knowledge, explore cutting-edge advancements, and inspire the next generation of tech pioneers. Through diverse and inclusive events, we aim to drive meaningful conversations, create opportunities for learning, and empower individuals and organizations to harness technology for positive change.

Past Speakers

Dr. Raj Patel

Raj Patel is the CEO and co-founder of NexaTech Solutions, one of the fastest-growing cloud infrastructure companies in the world. Known for his dynamic leadership and forward-thinking strategies, Raj has led NexaTech to achieve significant milestones in cloud computing and cybersecurity. With over 20 years of experience in the tech industry, he has been recognized as one of Forbes' "Top 50 Tech Leaders to Watch" and is a passionate advocate for digital transformation in emerging markets.

Topic at TechCon 2024: "Cloud and Cybersecurity: Scaling Secure Systems for Tomorrow"

Dr. Sophia Martine - AI & Ethics Expert

Dr. Sophia Martinez is a leading researcher and professor in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Ethics at Stanford University. With over 15 years of experience, she specializes in the ethical implications of AI in various sectors, from healthcare to autonomous vehicles. Dr. Martinez is a vocal advocate for responsible AI development, ensuring that technologies remain inclusive and fair. She has published numerous papers on AI fairness and transparency and is often consulted by governments and tech companies on AI regulations.

Topic at TechCon 2024: "Ethical AI: Building Responsible Systems for the Future"
